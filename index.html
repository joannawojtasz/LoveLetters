Welcome to Love letters!

Love letters is an interactive game for kids who want to learn to recognize letters and to spell. Game offers two levels of difficulty:

  • recognize first letters (4-6 years)
  • spelling bee(older kids)

Love letters rules

  • Player needs to name object on the picture. Depending on a level the goal is too choose correctly first letter of items name or spell the name correctly.
  • One game consists of 10 questions.
  • Player start with 3 lifes, a wrong choice of letter causes life loss.
  • Loosing a life or answering all questions ends the game.
  • On advanced level player has 5 tries to corect the spelling after you lost a life.
